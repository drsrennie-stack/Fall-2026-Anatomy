Read me first How this class works ▸

This course is flipped: you meet each concept first on your own, then we spend class time using it. Here is the rhythm every week:

The night before
1 · Prepare

Watch the assigned online concept lecture and do the guided notes in Canvas. This is your pre-work, always due the night before class.

In class
2 · Clarify + check

A short interactive lecture on the tricky, high-yield parts. Quick iCheck / tCheck questions during it confirm you prepped and keep you engaged. They count.

In class
3 · Engage

We apply it, cases, clinical correlations, walk-through maps, or a game. On TBL days this is a full Team-Based Learning event with IRAT / TRAT.

The one rule that makes it work: do the pre-work the night before. If you walk in having watched the lecture, the checks are easy and class actually makes sense. Click any day below to see exactly what to prep.
